Baobab Beach Resort

2 NIGHTS FLYING PACKAGE

Located on the world famous Diani Beach, The Baobab Beach Resort and Spa is set within 80 acres of tropical gardens, overlooking the Indian Ocean. An oasis of tranquillity and serenity, the Resort's picturesque location is nestled on a coral headland overlooking vast stretches of golden beach. Shaded by indigenous coastal vegetation and exotic coconut palms the resort stretches over 500 metres of golden beach front offering the ultimate luxury beach holiday.

Guestrooms have been specifically designed to provide paramount comfort and luxury for all clients. Guests may utilise leisure facilities across the resort including 3 swimming pools, dining areas and much more.

Baobab Beach Resort and Spa is renowned as Kenya's leading all-inclusive resort and takes pride in an exemplary level of service and attention to detail. The staff are on-hand to ensure your stay is both relaxing and unforgettable.

Day 1

Arrive at Wilson Airport for your scheduled flight to Ukunda.

The flight is approximately 1 hr 15 minutes. Upon arrival at Ukunda airstrip‚ you will be met by the hotel representative and transferred to the Baobab Beach Resort for check-in and overnight.

Day 2

Spend the day at leisure with a variety of activities to choose from such as walking on the white beaches‚ boat rides‚ scuba diving‚ water sports activities‚ golf at the leisure lodge or enjoying the daylight by the beach side. All meals and overnight at the hotel.

Day 3

Check out after breakfast or lunch and transfer to Ukunda airport for your flight back to Nairobi.
