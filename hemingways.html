Watamu

Hemingways

2 Nights Flying Package(BOTH RESIDENT & NON-RESIDENT)

Set on Watamu beach on the Indian Ocean coast, this relaxed resort in Watamu Marine National Park is 6 km from Gede Ruins, a 13th-century Arab settlement, and 5 km from Mida Creek.

The bright, airy rooms with balconies and ocean views have tile floors and simple wood furnishings. Suites add living rooms, and some have terraces. Upgraded suites feature 2nd bathrooms, dining areas and kitchenettes.

There are 2 restaurants, 2 outdoor pools and a poolside bar, plus a spa, a gym and a tennis court. Activities such as big game fishing, scuba diving and kite-surfing are available, as is breakfast.

Day 1

Arrive at Wilson Airport for your scheduled flight to Malindi.

Upon arrival at Malindi Airport, you will be met by the hotel representative and transferred to the hotel for check-in and overnight.

Day 2

Spend the day at leisure with a variety of activities to choose from such as walking on the white beaches‚ boat rides‚ scuba diving‚ water sport activities‚ relaxing around the swimming pool or enjoying the daylight by the beachside.

Day 3

Check out and transferred to Malindi airport for your scheduled flight to Nairobi - Wilson.
