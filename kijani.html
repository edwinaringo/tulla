Kijani Hotel

Lamu

2 NIGHT FLYING PACKAGE

Perfectly situated on the shores of the Indian Ocean in Shela Village on Lamu Island. You’ll find Kijani Hotel along the seafront nestled among swaying palms and makuti roofs, located just a short stroll away from the main village jetty.

Kijani welcomes you. With a freshwater pool, a relaxed rooftop restaurant, and beautiful gardens, Kijani offers all you need to immerse yourself in these unique surroundings.

The buildings showcase the best features of Swahili architecture and design. Throughout the hotel, you’ll find traditional Swahili furniture offering a perfect spot for an afternoon nap on your breezy terrace. While the 3000sq ft of fragrant tropical gardens await your exploration.

Day 1

Arrive at Wilson Airport for your scheduled flight to Lamu. The flight is approximately 1 hr 30 minutes.

Upon arrival at Manda Airport‚ you will be met by The Majlis resort representative and take a short boat transfer to Kijani Hotel for check-in and overnight. Lunch and dinner are not included.

Day 2

Spend the day at leisure with a variety of activities to choose from such as walking on the white beaches‚ boat rides‚ scuba diving‚ water sports activities‚ relaxing around the swimming pool or enjoying the daylight by the beachside.

Day 3

Check out and transferred by boat to Manda Airport for your scheduled flight to Wilson Airport in Nairobi.

What is included

2 nights accommodation in a classic room on half board basis.

Return flights from Wilson Airport to Manda Airport.

Return airport transfers from Manda Airport to Kijani Hotel.

What is not included

Drinks and other extras

Airport transfers in Nairobi (can be arranged on request)

Single room supplement.
