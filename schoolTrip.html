School Travel Program

An international school trip is an effective way to give students a break from the classroom and allow them to learn in new and engaging environments. Some of the benefits of educational travel include improved critical thinking skills, stronger teacher-student relationships, and an expanded worldview.

Increasingly, it's becoming more and more common for teachers and schools to recruit school travel companies to help organize educational trips abroad. Experts believe this is due to the increased pressure on the education system to produce global citizens that are adaptable and ready for the modern workplace.

Working with the Tulla School travel program team to organize an educational trip can be extremely beneficial, especially for your first time. We are travel specialists and know the logistics of traveling with a large group of students and can organize seamless itineraries with time, travel, accommodation, and educational activities all considered. They can also help with your school trip risk assessment, and health and safety planning.

We exist for a reason. We know what we are doing and want to work with you to create a fun, once-in-a-lifetime learning experience for your students. Here are the benefits of the Tulla School Travel Team to help organize your next school tour.

We know how to prepare for the worst

News sources frequently report on the rising global threat level and expose us to the consequences unpredictable weather is having across the globe. This means having a solid contingency plan in place is crucial when planning an international school trip.

One of the best ways Tulla Travel can support you is by ensuring every scenario that could take place is considered and considered again.

We have the knowledge and years of experience with connections to support you on the ground if an unlikely event occurs.

We can easily change accommodations, flights, and activities so that your trip runs smoothly. Nobody wants to sit on hold with airlines or tour operators for hours, especially with a group of school children. Using Tulla Trave ensures this doesn't happen and everything runs seamlessly.

Tulla School Travel teams takes the stress out of organizing a school trip

As a teacher or a parent, your time is valuable, and planning a trip can be an added stress in your already busy life.

Planning a school trip is very different from a personal vacation, you'll need to consider finding hotels that can accommodate everyone, restaurants that cater to different dietary requirements, what activities are worthwhile, how much time you'll need to get between activities, and so much more.

We have the experience and training required to critically look at your school's needs and deliver a seamless itinerary at the best price. We make sure each aspect of your trip meets your specific requirements so you and your students can enjoy the experience.
