Tsavo

East & West

2 NIGHTS PACKAGE Attractions

The wildlife i.e. vast herds of Tsavo elephants, rhinos, buffalo, lions, leopards, waterbucks, oryx, gerenuk, and the prolific bird life features 500 recorded species.

Birdwatching.

Panoramic views.

Unique game viewing experience.

Buffet meals.

Day 1

Pick up from the hotel/location of residence in Mombasa in the morning by our driver and travel towards Tsavo East national park which is renowned for large numbers of elephants and man-eating lions.

Begin the game drive en route to Aruba Dam (A water hole where many animals come to drink water). Physical attractions for the 3-day Tsavo safari include the Tsavo River, Yatta Plateau which is 300 km2, Mudanda Rock, Laggardâ€™s Falls, Voi seasonal River, and Aruba Dam. Lunch, dinner, and overnight stay will be in Tsavo East Lodge.

Day 2

Have a pre-breakfast game drive (it's the best time to witness a lion hunt) and then head for Tsavo West after breakfast. We shall have a short game drive through the park as we reach the lodge for lunch.

After lunch we shall visit the most iconic attraction in the park; Mzima Springs. The crystal-clear water is filtered through the volcanic rocks of the Chyulu Hills just to the north. Shaded by majestic fig and acacia trees, the lakes swarm with fish, large crocodiles, and some big pods of hippos. Lunch, dinner, and overnight stay in Tsavo West Lodge.

Day 3

After a scrumptious breakfast we shall visit the Rhino Sanctuary and have a short game drive. Then head back home, leaving the park before 11 am.
