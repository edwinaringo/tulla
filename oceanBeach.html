Ocean Beach

Resort and Spa

2 NIGHTS FLYING PACKAGE (BOTH RESIDENTS & NON-RESIDENTS)

Ocean Beach Resort & Spa offers contemporary luxury accommodations, a world-class spa, gourmet restaurants, natural beauty and an indefinable hospitality experience. A distinguished member of Leading Hotels of the World and Leading Spas of the World, Ocean Beach Resort & Spa has received numerous awards including Trip Advisor's Travellers' Choice Awards for 2014; Rated Number 1 in Kenya for Luxury Hotels and Number 9 in all of Africa.

Individual and luxuriously appointed suites are spread around the beautiful garden overlooking the beach and the sand dunes; the main building area is the social centre of the resort, with abundant, cool and elegant spaces in which to soak in the casual ambience of the resorts' historic backdrop.

Day 1

Arrive at Wilson Airport for your scheduled flight to Malindi. The flight is approximately 1 hr 30 minutes.

Upon arrival at Malindi Airport, you will be met by the hotel representative and taken to the Hotel for check-in. Rest of the afternoon at leisure to enjoy the beach

Day 2

Spend the day at leisure with a variety of activities to choose from such as walking on the white beaches, boat rides, dhow dinner, scuba diving, water sport activities‚ relaxing around the swimming pool‚ or enjoying the daylight by the beachside. All meals at the lodge.

Day 3

Check out and transferred by boat to Malindi Airport for your scheduled flight to Wilson Airport in Nairobi.

What is included

2 nights accommodation on half board basis

Return flights from Wilson airport to Malindi Airport

Return airport transfers to the hotel

20% discount on selected spa treatments

What is not included

Single room supplement. (Available on request)

Lunch and dinner

Any other item not mentioned above.
