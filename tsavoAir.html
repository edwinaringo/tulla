Experience Tsavo from the

Air

Tsavo Park is the biggest in Kenya covering 218218000 square kilometres and is one of the World's largest wildlife reserves. Divided into two halves roughly the same size Tsavo West and Tsavo East. The dividing line is the road and railway between Nairobi and Mombasa.

MOMBASA AIR SAFARI lands only in TSAVO WEST at Finch Hatton's Luxury Camp.

TSAVO WEST is the more visited area of Tsavo with numerous hills and rivers volcanic lava flows and also tall grass and woodland. The developed area lies between the Tsavo River and the Mombasa road. Here the landscape is varied and often very beautiful and from certain sites Mt. Kilimanjaro may appear on the horizon. Tsavo is well known for Elephant and the red soil of this terrain has created the myth of the Red Elephant.

Day 1: Mombasa or Diani to Tsavo West

Early morning pick up from your hotel / Residence. Transfer to the main airport for your scheduled flight to Tsavo west National park. Flight duration is approximately 40 minutes by a non-stop flight.

Transfer to the lodge or Luxury camp.

Proceed for a game viewing en-route to your lodge for check-in and lunch. There shall be moments of relaxation after lunch.

Day 2 Tsavo west air safari afternoon game drive will include visits to Mzima springs and Shetani lava flow. Its from this point you will be able to see Mount Kilimanjaro. Game drives while on an air safari package are not hectic.

Dinner and overnight in a luxury tented Camp or lodge

Day 2: Tsavo West – Kenyan Coast

Game drive shall start early before breakfast. Breakfast. Thereafter proceed with your game viewing exploring this vast natural phenomenon where members of the big five exists.

The morning game viewing is exciting. It at this time when animal are most active. You will witness the unfolding events in a natural wildlife habitat as you catch up with the early grazers and the predators. Return to your lodge| luxury camp for Lunch.

After lunch, transfer to the airstrip for your scheduled return flight. Met at the airport and transfer back to your sea port Hotel or Residence.
