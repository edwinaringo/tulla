What to take with you

Drinking water, National ID/Passport picnic items and packed snacks/lunch. Also useful are: binoculars, camera, hat, sunscreen, sunglasses and swimming kits.

Day 1

Pick up at Huduma Center (GPO Mombasa) at 6AM.

Depart Mombasa to Sarova Salt Lick Game Lodge.

Have lunch at Sarova Salt Lick Game Lodge.

After Lunch begin the game drive with a chance to spot; Elephant, Buffalo, Lions, Zebras, Gazelles and many other kinds of beautiful birds and animals.

Dinner and overnight stay at Salt Lick Game Lodge.

Day 2

Early morning game drive at 6AM-8AM.

Breakfast at 8:30 AM.

Game drive again after breakfast.

Leave early before lunch.

Have lunch in Voi.

Depart to Mombasa and arrive late evening.

What is included

Round trip transportation in a comfortable 8-seater safari tour van.

Game drives.

All statutory taxes.

English speaking Tour Driver/Guide.

What is not included

Lunch at Voi (Day 2).

Items of personal nature.
