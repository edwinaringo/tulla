El Karama

Lodge

2 NIGHTS FLYING PACKAGE (RESIDENT & NON-RESIDENT)

Designed, built, and operated by owners Murray and Sophie Grant, the award-winning El Karama eco-lodge sits on 14,000 acres of private wilderness on the banks of the Ewaso Nyiro River frequented by a vast array of wildlife – including four of the Big 5. El Karama’s cottages and bandas exude a ‘home away from home feeling that resonates throughout the lodge with a focus on family, sustainability, and homegrown produce. Explore the wilderness on a guided bush walk, spend the evening under the African stars in a suspended star tent, relax by the eco swimming pool, or tour the ranch, dairy and shamba where the majority or ingredients are sourced for the delicious farm to fork meals served at the lodge.

Day 1

Arrive at Wilson Airport for your scheduled flight to Nanyuki.

The flight is approximately 1 hour 30 minutes to Nanyuki. Upon arrival at the airstrip‚‚ you will be met by the El Karama driver guide. Followed by an hour transfer to the lodge‚ arriving in time for check-in. Lunch will be served with rest and high tea to follow. Embark on an afternoon game drive in an open vehicle. Later return to the lodge‚ enjoy a drink around the log fire as you socialize with other guests from all over the world. Gather around the dining table for a seated and served dinner enjoying the sounds of the African night.

Day 2

You will set out just before sunrise in search of game as you enjoy the richness of 14‚000 acres of private land and views of over 200‚000 acres.

El Karama is one of Laikipia's most magical habitats. Home to several endemic and endangered species including the Grevy Zebra‚ Reticulated Giraffe‚ Elephant‚ Buffalo‚ Lion‚ Leopard‚ Cheetah‚ Hippo‚ Greater Kudu‚ Aardwolf‚ Aardvark‚ African Hunting Dogs to name but some of the wildlife found here. The landscapes are diverse‚ incredibly beautiful and the safari experience unforgettable. Our team of guides is as curious about the trees and birds. Plants and Insects as they are the bigger creatures and they will make sure you have the time to absorb and connect completely on foot‚ in vehicles and even on horseback. Lunch will be served at the lodge‚ later you may choose to head out on a game drive or enjoy a guided walk led by a trained guide. Return to camp for dinner and overnight.
