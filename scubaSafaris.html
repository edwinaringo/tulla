Dive & Safari Packages

Kenya offers some of the most amazing experiences that any person could wish for. It is famous for its World-renowned African wildlife, found in its numerous National Parks, as well as the spectacular vistas of the Great Rift Valley, the peaks of Mt Kenya and Mt Kilimanjaro and the fabulous beaches and coral reefs found on its Indian Ocean coastline.

Tulla Adventures offer a once-in-a-lifetime experience. We offer bespoke dive and safari packages that involve a few days on safari in the African savannah, a stay on the magnificent Diani Beach, and several days scuba diving. If we don’t offer a specific package to fit your needs then we can custom-build one for you based on what you want to do.

THE SANDS AT CHALE ISLAND

The Sands at Chale Island provides the perfect backdrop for a scuba diving adventure, with the blissful Indian Ocean, teeming with life literally on your door step, the island also offers mangroves and a coastal forest flourishing with bird and insect life. Nature lovers are in for a real treat here.

Located 600 metres from the mainland, The Sands at Chale Island is a fantastic bolt-hole getaway, perfect for those that like the idea of remote location.

For your coastal tour of Kenya, we recommend the coral gardens of Kisite Mpunguti Marine Park, one of the country’s premier snorkelling sites. The site is especially engaging for guests who desire up-close sightings of dolphins and migratory humpback whales. The diversity includes over 360 fish species and 45 types of corals.

Turtle lovers will savour every moment on the beaches of Watamu Marine Park where the turtles nest. Other worthwhile sites in Kenya are Mombasa Marine Park, Malindi Marine Park, Kiunga Marine National Reserve, Manda Toto and Kiniyka. Tanzania’s Zanzibar Island is not only a cultural treasure, but the waters surrounding the island are alive with marine species, such as eels, octopus and seahorses, as well as many vibrant fishes like scorpionfish, zebrafish, pufferfish, glassfish, parrotfish, black triggerfish, sweetlips and more.

Prime spots to snorkel in Zanzibar are at Mnemba Atoll and Chumbe Marine Park.
