The Great Wilderbeasts

Migration

The migration is one of the most booked safaris in Kenya.

Every year two million wildebeest and zebra complete the 1800 mile circuit from the Ngorongoro Conservation Area in Tanzania, through the Serengeti, across the border into Kenya's Maasai Mara and back again. This is considered as the the greatest wildlife show on earth by many explorers.
