Tawi Lodge

Amboseli

2 NIGHTS FLYING PACKAGE (BOTH RESIDENT & NON-RESIDENT)

The picturesque Tawi Lodge lies on a beautiful private concession at the foot of Mount Kilimanjaro, only ten minutes away from the gate to Amboseli National Park. This eco-friendly lodge offers magnificent views from both its main guest areas and its beautifully appointed cottages. Each cottage boasts romantic four-poster beds, a lovely ensuite bathroom with a Victorian bath and a shower, a fireplace and a personal bar. Butler service is provided for that added personal touch.

Day 1

Arrive at Wilson Airport for your scheduled morning or afternoon flight to Amboseli. The flight is approximately 40 minutes.

On arrival, you will be picked up from the airstrip and transferred to Tawi lodge for check-in ‚ where you will be greeted by the beauty of Africa’s largest mountain‚ Mount Kilimanjaro. Later have lunch‚ relax in the afternoon before proceeding on a shared game drive.

Dinner and overnight at the camp

Day 2

You will set out just before sunrise in search of game as you enjoy the richness of the Park's wildlife and endless savannah. Amboseli is best known for its unrivaled views of Kilimanjaro and the local elephant population. There are over 1000 elephants in the park ecosystem ‚ featuring some of the largest in Africa.

After the game drive, you will be treated to a full bush breakfast‚ al fresco‚ in the middle of the plains‚ surrounded by wildlife. Return to the lodge‚ where you will have time to relax on your room balcony reading a book with an impressive view of Africa's highest mountain. After lunch‚ you can choose to proceed on another game drive or extra activities offered by the lodge.

Day 3

After the morning game drive, you will be transferred to the airstrip for your scheduled flight back to Nairobi.

What is included

2 nights full board accommodation in a luxury cottage

Return flights from Wilson airport

2 shared game drives per day

Return airstrip transfers in Amboseli

Guided walks

What is not included

Airport transfers in Nairobi (can be arranged on request)
