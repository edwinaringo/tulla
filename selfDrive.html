LUXURY SELF DRIVE

SUGGESTED ITINERARY OVERVIEW

Locations: Namib Desert, Damaraland and Etosha: Best to do this: Best between May to September but January to April are also interesting months.

Style: See Namibia’s best locations from some of their most luxurious and intimate camps and lodges

For who: For those discerning clients who value being pampered whilst taking in some of Africa’s most stunning vistas

Why we love it: With these camps you not only pay for the utter luxury and service they provide, but for their locations which in all three cases is magical and absolutely blew us away with the landscapes and huge variety of scenery.

2 x nights LITTLE KULALA

Including all meals, most drinsk and all desert activities (walking, drives, sundowners and much more). This camp is nothing short of stunning with mesmerising views out into the desert. Relax by the pool or soak up the desert life around you – this place is magical.

2 x nights DORO NAWAS

Including all meals, local drinks and all game viewing activities. If you’re lucky whilst staying at this homely camp you might see the elusive and heavily endangered Desert Rhino. As well as camp game drives you can take your own 4×4 out whenever you wish.

3 x nights LITTLE ONGAVA

Including all meals, local drinks and all game viewing. Enjoy the wildlife spectacle which is Etosha – big cats galore!

This itinerary includes car hire of a small 4×4. For more information on the details of this itinerary do not hesitate to contact us. This itinerary works with any of our camps in the Namib, Damaraland or Etosha. You might also consider flying onwards to Mozambique or Mauritius for a beach holiday.
