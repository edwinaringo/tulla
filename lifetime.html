Days 1-5: 20 DEGRÉS SUD, MAURITIUS

4 nights at 20 Degrés Sud in a beachfront suite on a full board basis.

Push the big oak door and enter a wonderful haven of peace. Coconut cocktail for the heart, beautiful landscape for the memories, we’re taking care of you for this special occasion. Our boutique hotel is made for romantic breaks, intimate, calm and cosy, you’ll find a true feeling of wellness and happiness within our walls. For your lovely honeymoon, we’ll provide an excellent quality of services tailored by your needs and wants… Just ask, the 20 Degrés Sud family is here for you.

In the heart of a coconut grove, this boutique hotel inspired by a Mauritian Creole style is decorated entirely by the prestigious Belgian decorator, Flamant. Member of “Relais et Chateaux”, the hotel 20 ° Sud is recommended for adults and does not admit children under 12 years old. Everything is done to make your stay in Grand Baie particularly pleasant.

You will be staying in one of the suites at this intimate and discreet hotel. Unique and personalized by theme, they are distributed in one-storey buildings and face the lagoon. Some of them even have a hot tub or a small relaxation pool for added privacy

At the Secret Spa, our art is the rituals of well-being. We do not only take care of your skin, we do take care of the person and make your inner beauty be revealed by the magic hands of our therapists together with our branded products of Comfort Zone. Our higher purpose is to help and encourage people to take care of themselves and to live more soulfully.

Alternatively: DAY 1 – 5: ANJAJAVY LE LODGE, MADAGASCAR

4 nights at Anjajavy Le Lodge in a Seaview Villa on a full board basis

Experience the best of Madagascar wildlife, scenery, beaches and botany, encapsulated at beautiful Anjajavy at the west coast of Madagascar.

Nestled in lush vegetation on a peninsula on the west coast of Madagascar, Anjajavy Lodge overlooks a bay and is backed by a natural reserve. Guest accommodation is provided in 24 sea-facing villas, all air-conditioned and each featuring a terrace, living room, salon, bedroom, private bathroom with a bathtub, separate toilets, and one extra room with sleeper couches upstairs.

The onsite restaurant/bar offers an a la carte menu and guests can request private dinners on the beach or in their accommodations. There is also a bar, swimming pool, and private beach. A wide range of beach and water activities can be enjoyed at the lodge and surrounds.

DAYS 5 – 6: FAIRLAWNS, JOHANNESBURG

1 night at Fairlawns Boutique Hotel & Spa in a luxury courtyard suite on a bed and breakfast basis. With close attention to detail and exceptional service, Fairlawns creates an intimate atmosphere where some of the globe's most vibrant personalities converge. The 41 chic, upscale suites are individually decorated in varying themes ranging from Eastern influence to French provincial, each featuring every desired modern amenity.

The hotel also caters to a variety of culinary tastes with an array of elegant dining experiences to choose from. Located in Johannesburg - South Africa's largest city and gateway to the rest of Africa - Fairlawns is situated just 6 km from central Sandton, and 20 km from the Johannesburg city centre to offer the upmarket traveller a delicate mix of luxury and convenience.

The Courtyard Suites are situated in Palladian style buildings that forms an elegant courtyard graced by a formal garden. Each suite is decorated with a specific theme in mind and is named accordingly, lending unique elements of fun to each space.

The Spa guarantees to rejuvenate, relax and restore in the exquisite bamboo forest gardens of the Balinese-style Spa at Fairlawns. With a range of therapies and signature Asian-inspired treatments, a team of skilled therapists are able to bring you back to a state of calm and natural wellbeing.

DAYS 6 – 10: DUBA PLAINS CAMP, OKAVANGO DELTA

4 nights at Duba Plains Camp in a luxury suite on a full board basis. Duba Plains Camp is a wildlife connoisseur’s getaway to the Okavango Delta. Built on a wooded island, connected to the prolific wildlife area by an elaborate set of bridges, the camp is the base of many famous wildlife documentaries by National Geographic filmmakers Dereck and Beverly Joubert. The intimate camp consists of five traditional-style suites and the 2-bedroom Duba Plains Suite. All sit on raised decking, each with views of the surrounding floodplain and the steady stream of wildlife parading past.

Each suite has a lounge area, a private veranda, beautifully appointed en-suite facilities consisting of flush loo, double basins and indoor and outdoor showers and luxurious baths.

Guests will be able to enjoy their own private plunge pool and outdoor under cover sala/day rest area. The main area consists of a large raised dining room, and a comfortable lounge with private diningBareas, wine cellar, library, and chef’s kitchen with ample space for guests to either find a privacy or come together. The camp’s centre-point is the gathering place of all bush experiences, the fire place on an extended deck into the swamp. Duba Plain’s Toyota Land Cruisers are uniquely designed to traverse the floodplains. Duba Plains offers Botswana’s best land safari as well as an Okavango Delta experience, from wildlife viewing and birding or fishing from the boat, to walking and exciting drives with knowledgeable guides and friendly hosts.

Because this is a very special occasion, the camp will spoil you with private dinners, bubble baths and unique experiences and surprises throughout your stay.
